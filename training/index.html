Master Plan/Training

Training

Skills built deliberately rather than hoped for passively

Each subsection follows the same internal structure — Overview, Prerequisites, Resources (tiered), Practice, Milestones, Current state, Next target, Log. Progress is tracked using a five-stage framework: Introduced → Deliberate → Supported → Default → Integrated.

01
Voice
Pitch, resonance, intonation, integration — the largest and most structured subsection.
02
Posture & Movement
Standing, walking, sitting, transitions, carrying. Elbows closer to torso.
03
Mannerisms & Gesture
Hand use, head movement, eye contact, facial engagement.
04
Social Dynamics
Listening patterns, space negotiation, register shifts, hedging.
05
Hair Handling
Tucking, moving out of face, tying up, wind management. Waits on hair/wig.
06
Presentation-adjacent
Heels, purse management, skirt management, makeup application speed.
